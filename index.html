Italian, Indian, Chinese cuisine with Bar concepts. It is family owned enterprise with a consistent record
of growth internationally with other business. The Taste of Nepal is pioneering force in brand expanding
using its exceptional knowledge and unforgettable guests experience to expand at pace.
