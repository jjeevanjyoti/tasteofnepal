About Us

Taste of Nepal is a dynamic multi cuisine restaurant which serves Authentic Nepali cuisine, Americana,
Italian, Indian, Chinese cuisine with Bar concepts. It is family owned enterprise with a consistent record
of growth internationally with other business. The Taste of Nepal is pioneering force in brand expanding
using its exceptional knowledge and unforgettable guests experience to expand at pace.

Our restaurant offers world class cuisine

Food is essential, satisfying not only our basic nutritional needs but also bringing us together for
shared communal experiences. Preparing, sharing and eating food together is at the heart of what it means to be human.

Foresight, energy and a passion to be the best drive us to explore new opportunities in different nations around the globe to form strong and effective business partnerships built on trust, integrity and mutual benefit. We are proud of the expertise that exists within Taste of Nepal and we work hard to maintain our reputation as an employer of choice.
